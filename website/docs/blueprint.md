## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, calming blue (#3498db) to evoke the essence of water and environmental consciousness. Avoid teal.
- Background color: Desaturated light blue (#E0F2F7), creating a soft, unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: A vibrant analogous cyan (#489FB5), is used to draw attention to important interactive elements. Avoid teal.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (sans-serif) to convey elegance and a unique artistic touch.
- Body font: 'Alegreya' (serif) for a readable, sophisticated textual feel.
- Hand-drawn style icons to give a custom, artistic impression. Show natural beauty of water.
- Fluid and organic layouts, asymmetrical designs with curved lines for a watery, natural feel.
- Water-like animations: subtle wave effects and flowing transitions.
